Biographical Information

Robert was born in Indianapolis, Indiana and grew up in Avon, Indiana. He spent his high school years as a member of the Avon High School Marching Band and graduated from Avon High School in 2007. He started his studies at Purdue University that year. During his undergraduate career, Robert interned at Goodwin and Marshall in Grapevine, Texas working on land development projects in three states and twice received the Undergraduate Cementitious Materials Research Award. He graduated with his BSCE with an emphasis in construction materials in 2011. Robert began his graduate studies upon graduation and earned his MSCE in August 2013. He continued as a doctoral student studying construction materials at Purdue University with Professor Jason Weiss and is planning to graduate in August 2016.
