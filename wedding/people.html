The Groomsmen

Albert

Albert has been one of my best friends since we met working at Purdue together. I have a lot of good memories with Albert, including going on spring break vacation to Florida, helping to renovate his sister's house, and spending lots of time together as we worked our way through grad school. I had the opportunity to move into his place during grad school, and I would say he is a 5-star roommate. I'm thankful to Albert because he has helped me realize how important is it to have fun in your life, and some of my most fun memories have included times that we have shared together. I asked Albert to be one of my groomsman because I always have fun when we see each other, and reconnecting with Albert is always so easy. I'm excited for Albert because he will be getting married in July to a wonderful and kind lady, and I'm so thankful that him and Kirstyn will be able to be here to celebrate with Luisa and me.
