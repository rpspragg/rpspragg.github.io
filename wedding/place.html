Corvallis is...

a town of a population of approximately fifty thousand. It is located about two hours south from Portland, Oregon. This page will provide information about getting to Corvallis and places to stay while you are here.

Our place is located at 210 SW Jefferson Ave, Apartment 10, Corvallis, OR.

Where to fly in to

There are three airports that are an option to fly into when coming to visit us in Corvallis. The easiest is Portland International (PDX airport code). A closer regional airport is available in Eugene (EUG airport code), although this might prove slightly more expensive. A third more distant option is flying in to Seattle, Washingtnon, (SEA airport code) but this option will require about a five hour drive south. It might be a good option if you want to see more of the Pacific Northwest!

How to get to Corvallis

From SEA or EUG airports, your best bet will be to rent a car.

From PDX, there are a couple of options. The first is renting a car. The second option is to take an uber or taxi to the Amtrak station in Portland, and take the Amtrak train to Albany, Oregon. Tickets for the train are about $17 each way. You can find more info at AMTRAK and searching between Portland, OR (PDX) and Albany, OR (ALY). If you do this option, let us know and we will find a way to get you from the Amtrak station in Albany to our place here in Corvallis, it is only about a ten minute drive. The third option is taking a shuttle, ther is the HUT Shuttle and Oregon Express Shuttle that goes between PDX and Corvallis.

Where to stay

There are a few different hotels around Coravllis, you can find a complete list here. We will be partying downtown after the wedding ceremony, so the Holiday Inn and the Super 8 would be good options. If you have questions about a specific place, give us a call!
